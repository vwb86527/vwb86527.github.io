			<p>The NCAA Division 1 College Football Playoff is held once a year, aiming to feature the nations 4 best college football teams. The teams are chosen based on their overall record, strength of schedule, and over various statistics, by a committee of past athletic directors and coaches. The teams are seeded 1-4, resulting in the 1 seed playing the 4 seed and the 2 seed playing the 3 seed in the first round. The winners of each game meet in the championship to determine the best team in college football.</p>
